# Sprints (private)

_Private sprint planning + retro scratch. Use for personal / managerial sprint reflection that's separate from the team's planning artifacts._

## Use this for

- Pre-planning thoughts before sprint kickoff
- Private retro notes (raw observations before the team retro)
- Capacity / load tracking that isn't team-visible
- Trial-balloon ideas for future sprint goals

## Use `team_notes` or `team_hub` instead when

- The team should see the planning input
- The retro is happening and needs shared notes
- Capacity allocation is a discussion topic

## Default post target

`post_targets.sprint → private_sprints` (private context)

Override in `jstack.config.json` if your team plans entirely in shared space.
