# Notes

_Working notes and private decision log. Half-step between Scratchpad and team-visible Documents._

## Sections to consider

- **Decisions** — private decision log; promote significant ones to ADR via `/jstack:notion adr`
- **Observations** — patterns, hypotheses, things to watch
- **Drafts** — content that may go to a team channel, doc, or announcement once polished
- **Reading queue** — articles, papers, docs to digest

## When to promote

| From here | To | Trigger |
|---|---|---|
| Decision note | ADR | Affects architecture or future trade-offs |
| Draft | Team Notes / Announcement | Ready for team eyes |
| Observation | Wiki section | Validated and useful long-term |
| Reading note | KB page | Worth referencing in onboarding |
